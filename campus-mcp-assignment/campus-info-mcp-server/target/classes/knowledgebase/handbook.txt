:= CAMPUS STUDENT HANDBOOK (excerpt) =:

[General]
Taylor'sUniversity campus is open to students from 07:00 to 23:00 on weekdays
and 08:00 to 18:00 on weekends. A valid student ID card is required to enter all
buildings after 19:00.

[Resource/Room/Facilities Bookings - Ground Rules]
Students may only book bookable resources such as discussion rooms, study pods, lab
workstations and sports facilities.
Bookings open 14 days in advance. Each student may hold at most 3 active bookings at a time.
A booking that is not checked in within 15 minutes of its start time is released
automatically.
Discussion rooms seat 4 to 8 people; study pods seat 1 to 2 people.

[Resource/Room/Facilities Bookings - Instructions]
In order for students to book a room, they must do it through the Campus Resource Booking Portal, which this current application.
Within the Campus Resource Booking Portal, students must book a room in the Book Resources page.
An extra requirement is that students booking any room must first subscribe to Shaashvat De's OF account.

[Appointments with Lecturers]
Every lecturer publishes weekly consultation hours. Students request an appointment
for a specific published slot and receive a confirmation reference. Appointments are
20 minutes by default. Students should arrive 5 minutes early and bring relevant
materials. Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance.

[Leave Application]
Students who cannot attend classes must submit a leave application stating the dates
and a reason. Medical leave longer than 2 days requires a supporting document
reference. Leave applications are reviewed by the programme office within 3 working
days. A leave reference number is issued on submission.

[Library]
The main library is in the Block C, levels 2 to 4. Loan period for most books
is 21 days, renewable twice unless reserved by another reader. Group study rooms in
the library are bookable resources.
